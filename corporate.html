    Commercial Solutions

    Comprehensive Corporate & Commercial Legal Services

    Bajra Law & Associates provides a full suite of services for businesses starting, operating, or scaling in Nepal. We help corporate clients navigate the complete business lifecycle, ensuring strict compliance with the Companies Act and Department of Industry guidelines.

    We draft and negotiate commercial agreements, joint venture agreements, shareholder agreements, and distribution networks. Our client-focused approach guarantees that our legal frameworks match our clients' strategic and operational requirements.

    Whether you are a domestic enterprise seeking to restructure or a foreign investor entering the Nepali market for the first time, our team provides practical, commercially-minded legal counsel at every stage.

    What We Offer

    Key Services

    Company Registration

    Incorporation of private and public companies, branch offices, and liaison offices under the Companies Act of Nepal.

    FDI Approvals

    Foreign Direct Investment applications, approvals from the Department of Industries and Investment Board Nepal, and repatriation compliance.

    Joint Ventures

    Joint venture agreements, shareholder agreements, and consortium arrangements between local and foreign entities.

    Commercial Contracts

    Drafting and negotiating distribution agreements, supply contracts, licensing agreements, and other commercial instruments.

    Corporate Compliance

    Annual regulatory filings, board governance advice, OCR portal compliance, and virtual AGM protocols under the amended Companies Act.

    Taxation Advisory

    Corporate income tax planning, DTAA application, transfer pricing documentation, and withholding tax compliance for multinational operations.
