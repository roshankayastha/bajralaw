    Capacity Building

    At Bajra Law & Associates, we believe that legal empowerment is essential for sustainable growth and innovation. Our Capacity Building practice is designed to equip individuals, organizations, and institutions with the legal knowledge, practical skills, and confidence they need to navigate complex legal environments and make informed decisions.

    We provide tailored training programs, workshops, and advisory services that bridge the gap between legal theory and real-world application. Whether you are a corporation seeking to train your compliance team, an engineering faculty integrating IP education, or a government body building legislative capacity, we are here to help.

    Our Approach: We combine deep legal expertise with a passion for education and knowledge-sharing. Our programs are interactive, practical, and customized to the needs of each client. We believe that when people understand the law, they are better equipped to protect their interests, comply with regulations, and build with confidence.

    What We Offer

    Key Services

    1. Intellectual Property Training for Engineering Faculties and Technical Students

    • Custom-designed training programs for engineering colleges, technical institutes, and research institutions
    • Introduction to intellectual property concepts (patents, trademarks, copyrights, trade secrets, industrial designs)
    • Patent drafting and filing workshops for faculty members and researchers
    • Understanding patentability criteria, prior art searches, and freedom-to-operate analysis
    • IP management and commercialization strategies for academic inventions
    • Institutional IP policy development for universities and research centers
    • Interactive sessions, case studies, and hands-on drafting exercises

    2. Legal Awareness Programs for Professionals and Businesses

    • Workshops on key business laws (Companies Act, E-Commerce Act, FITTA, labor laws)
    • Compliance training for corporate legal and compliance teams
    • Contract drafting and negotiation skills for procurement and sales professionals
    • Data protection and privacy law awareness for technology companies
    • Risk management and regulatory compliance programs
    • Customized programs for industry associations and professional bodies

    3. Policy and Legislative Training for Government Officials

    • Legislative drafting and policy analysis workshops
    • Training on parliamentary procedures and law-making processes
    • Regulatory impact assessment and consultation skills
    • Constitutional and administrative law for public officials
    • Programs tailored to federal, provincial, and local government needs

    4. Know Your Law: Institutional Capacity Development

    • Understanding your legal rights and obligations
    • Guidance on environmental compliance and sustainability
    • Developing institutional legal frameworks and internal policies
    • Training board members and leadership on fiduciary duties and governance
    • Programs for NGOs, INGOs, and development partners operating in Nepal

    5. Research and Advisory Support

    • Legal research and policy analysis for decision-making
    • Development of legal compliance toolkits and guides
    • Best practices advisory on legal risk management
    • Support for developing institutional legal frameworks
    Featured Program

    Intellectual Property Training for Engineering Faculties

    Engineering and technical innovation are at the heart of Nepal's development. Yet, many engineers and researchers lack the legal knowledge needed to protect their inventions and navigate the IP landscape.

    Our IP training programs for engineering faculties and technical students are designed to:

    • Help researchers understand patentability criteria and the patent filing process
    • Enable faculty members to guide students in protecting their innovations
    • Assist institutions in developing IP policies and commercialization strategies
    • Foster a culture of innovation and IP awareness within technical institutions
    Format: Half-day to multi-day workshops, interactive sessions, case studies, and hands-on drafting exercises.
