    Legal Policy & Reform

    Research-Driven Legislative Review & Reform Advisory

    Bajra Law & Associates is at the forefront of legal research and statutory drafting in Nepal. We assist government bodies, international development agencies, non-profits, and corporate entities in conducting detailed comparative law studies, analyzing regulatory impacts, and drafting legislative frameworks.

    Our policy and legislative review practice combines academic rigor with practical commercial insights, helping shape modern, effective legal frameworks that promote innovation and protect public interest.

    As part of our transactional expertise, we also provide end-to-end Mergers & Acquisitions advisory services, conducting thorough legal due diligence, transaction documentation, and obtaining merger clearances under competition laws.

    What We Offer

    Key Services

    Comparative Legal Studies

    Conducting deep-dive research into global and regional legal frameworks to benchmark and advise on local law reforms.

    Statutory & Rule Drafting

    Drafting bills, local bylaws, model rules, and guidelines for federal, provincial, and local government institutions.

    Regulatory Impact Assessment

    Analyzing the socio-economic and legal implications of proposed regulations to ensure clarity, fairness, and ease of compliance.

    Institutional Reform Advisory

    Advising public sector entities, state commissions, and international agencies on reforming legal administration and regulatory compliance.

    M&A Due Diligence & Structuring

    Conducting comprehensive legal due diligence, transaction structuring, and negotiating share/asset purchase agreements in Nepal.

    Competition & Merger Clearance

    Securing merger approvals and regulatory compliance under Nepal's Competition Promotion and Market Protection Act.
