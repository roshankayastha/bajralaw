    Who We Are

    Legal architecture for innovation.

    Bajra Law & Associates is an independent institution that brings together legal practice, research, policy development, education, and strategic advisory at the intersection of technology, innovation, intellectual property, and international law.

    We are not a traditional corporate law firm. We are a research-driven practice that believes law is more than a mechanism of compliance, it is an architecture that enables innovation, strengthens institutions, and creates lasting impact.

    We provide strategic legal counsel to institutions, innovators, entrepreneurs, and individuals, helping them navigate complexity, protect their ideas, and build with confidence. Our clients range from local startups and engineering colleges to international organizations and foreign investors entering Nepal.
