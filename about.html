Our Profile

Dedicated to Corporate Legal Excellence

Founded with a vision to provide sophisticated, internationally-modeled legal services in Nepal, Bajra Law & Associates has grown into a leading corporate chambers. We advise global brands, development finance agencies, commercial banks, and local entrepreneurs on doing business in Nepal.

Our firm is deeply committed to high standards of integrity, continuous professional development, and timely communication. We translate complex Nepalese regulatory pathways into clear, practical legal advice, helping clients establish, protect, and scale their commercial interests.

Through our network of global partner firms, we also support Nepalese companies in regional expansions and cross-border commercial transactions.
