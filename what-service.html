    Match Your Goals to the Right Service

    Choosing the right legal service starts with understanding your primary need. We have designed our practice to offer a clear and direct path for every situation.

    Whether you require core strategic counsel on complex commercial contracting, a formal opinion to mitigate cross-border regulatory risks, or Capacity Building initiatives to empower your operational teams, our advisor guide helps you navigate to the correct service seamlessly.

    Strategic Legal Counsel

    For core advice on technology, IP, or business law, explore our Science & Technology or Litigation & Arbitration practices.

    A Formal Written Opinion

    For high-stakes decisions, cross-border transactions, or complex regulatory questions, you need a Legal Opinion.

    Guidance on Shaping the Law

    If you are a government body, international agency, or institution navigating legal reform, our Legislative & Policy Advisory is designed for you.

    To Empower Your Team

    For training, workshops, or "Know Your Law" programs, our Capacity Building services provide practical legal education and skills development.

    International Representation

    If you are a foreign company or law firm engaging with Nepal, our Foreign Legal Counsel service acts as your bridge to the local legal landscape.

    Help with Emerging Issues

    For advice on AI, data protection, climate law, or other new legal frontiers, our Evolving Areas of Law practice can help you anticipate and prepare for future challenges.

    Not sure? We offer an initial consultation to understand your goals and guide you to the right service.
