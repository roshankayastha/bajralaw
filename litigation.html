    Dispute Resolution

    Strategic Litigation & Arbitration Representation

    We represent clients before courts, arbitral tribunals, and other dispute resolution forums in a wide range of contentious matters. We provide strategic advocacy in litigation and arbitration proceedings while also recognizing the value of mediation and negotiated settlements where they serve our clients' interests more effectively.

    Our approach is guided by careful analysis, practical judgment, and a commitment to achieving outcomes that protect our clients' rights, interests, and long-term objectives.

    Practice Scope

    Areas of Practice

    Civil & Commercial Litigation

    • Representation in civil disputes before Nepal's District Courts, High Courts, and Supreme Court
    • Breach of contract claims and commercial torts
    • Banking and financial institution disputes
    • Insurance claims and coverage disputes
    • Debt recovery and enforcement of judgments

    Domestic & International Arbitration

    • Representation in arbitrations governed by the Arbitration Act, 2055 (1999)
    • International arbitration under ICC, UNCITRAL, and other institutional rules
    • Drafting of arbitration agreements and clauses
    • Appointment of arbitrators and procedural guidance
    • Enforcement of arbitral awards in Nepal and abroad

    Mediation & Negotiated Settlements

    • Strategic negotiation to avoid costly and protracted litigation
    • Mediation services for commercial, employment, and partnership disputes
    • Structured settlement agreements with enforceable terms
    • Confidential dispute resolution for sensitive business matters
    • Cost-effective alternatives to traditional courtroom battles
