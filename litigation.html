    Dispute Resolution

    Strategic Litigation & Arbitration Representation

    Our dispute resolution practice combines deep procedural knowledge with strategic commercial thinking. Bajra Law & Associates represents corporations, financial institutions, and individuals in civil and commercial litigation before all levels of courts in Nepal—from District Courts to the Supreme Court.

    We are highly experienced in domestic and international arbitration, including proceedings under the Arbitration Act of Nepal, ICC rules, and UNCITRAL rules. We provide full-service representation from pre-dispute strategy through enforcement of awards.

    Our litigation team maintains an unwavering focus on efficiency and outcome. We evaluate every dispute against its commercial context, advising clients on the most cost-effective path to resolution—whether through negotiation, mediation, arbitration, or court proceedings.

    What We Offer

    Key Services

    Commercial Litigation

    Representation in breach of contract, shareholder disputes, intellectual property infringement, and general commercial claims before Nepali courts.

    Domestic Arbitration

    Full advocacy in arbitration proceedings under the Arbitration Act 2055, including appointment of arbitrators, hearings, and award enforcement.

    International Arbitration

    Representation in ICC, SIAC, and UNCITRAL proceedings, including cross-border enforcement of foreign arbitral awards in Nepal under the New York Convention framework.

    Mediation & Conciliation

    Facilitation of out-of-court settlements through structured mediation under the Mediation Act, minimising litigation risk and cost for our clients.

    Writ Petitions

    Public interest litigation, constitutional writ petitions, and certiorari applications before the Supreme Court of Nepal on behalf of aggrieved parties.

    Enforcement Proceedings

    Recovery of court decrees, enforcement of arbitral awards, and asset recovery proceedings including injunctions and attachment orders.
