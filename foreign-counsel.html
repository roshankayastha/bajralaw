    Cross-Border Corporate Advisory

    Navigating Cross-Border Ventures & FDI in Nepal

    Bajra Law & Associates provides strategic legal counsel to multinational corporations, foreign direct investors, and global law firms entering or operating in Nepal. We help clients navigate complex foreign investment regulations, secure regulatory approvals, structure joint ventures, and handle central bank repatriation requirements.

    Our foreign legal counsel services ensure seamless cross-border transactions, offering clarity on tax treaties, double taxation agreements, and international compliance standards.

    We also maintain a strong expertise in Banking & Finance, enabling us to support foreign investors in project financing, syndicated lending, and securing loan approvals from the Nepal Rastra Bank (NRB) and relevant ministries.

    What We Offer

    Key Services

    Foreign Direct Investment (FDI)

    • Securing approvals from the Department of Industries (DOI) and Investment Board Nepal (IBN)
    • Joint venture structuring, equity limits, and business registration compliance
    • Minimum capital requirement advisory and statutory filings

    Cross-Border Structuring

    • Establishing subsidiaries, liaison offices, branch offices, and franchise structures
    • Inbound and outbound corporate entity structuring under the Companies Act
    • Joint Venture (JV) negotiations and detailed shareholder agreements

    NRB Repatriation & Forex Compliance

    • Obtaining central bank (Nepal Rastra Bank - NRB) approvals for profit repatriation
    • Securing foreign exchange setup and trade payment approvals
    • Foreign currency loan registration and compliance frameworks

    International Tax & Treaties

    • Structuring transactions under Double Taxation Avoidance Agreements (DTAA)
    • Transfer pricing reviews and international tax treaty alignment
    • Customs duty compliance and cross-border service taxation reviews

    Project & Trade Finance

    • Project finance structuring for infrastructure, hydro, and energy projects
    • Syndicated loan documentation with international Development Finance Institutions (DFIs)
    • Regulatory clearances for offshore borrowing and debt security creations

    Sovereign & Regulatory Risk

    • Evaluating political risk, regulatory shifts, and local compliance liabilities
    • Advising on commercial contracts with public entities and government ministries
    • Strategic threat assessment for cross-border investments and concession agreements
