    Foreign Legal Counsel

    As your Foreign Legal Counsel, we provide expert guidance on Nepali law to international clients, law firms, and institutions. Whether you are entering the Nepalese market, outsourcing to Nepal, or managing cross-border transactions, we ensure your interests are protected and your operations remain compliant.

    Our Approach

    We combine deep knowledge of Nepal's legal and regulatory framework with the responsiveness and transparency expected by global clients. We serve as your on-the-ground legal partner, providing clear, actionable advice that bridges the gap between international expectations and local realities.

    Practice Scope

    Our Services

    Legal Advisory for Foreign Companies Entering Nepal

    • Structuring foreign direct investment (FDI) and joint ventures
    • Company incorporation and registration under the Companies Act, 2063
    • Regulatory compliance across sectors (FDI limits, licensing, and reporting)
    • Foreign Investment and Technology Transfer Act (FITTA) compliance
    • Sector-specific advisory for technology, renewable energy, manufacturing, and tourism

    Outsourcing & Business Process Services

    • Legal framework for IT and BPO outsourcing contracts
    • Data protection and cross-border data transfer compliance
    • Drafting and review of service level agreements (SLAs) and outsourcing contracts
    • Intellectual property protection for outsourced innovation
    • Employment and contractor arrangements for offshore teams

    NGO & INGO Advisory

    • Registration and compliance for international non-governmental organizations
    • Legal framework for foreign funding and grant agreements
    • Regulatory reporting and government liaison
    • Project agreement drafting and review
    • Compliance with Social Welfare Council and other regulatory bodies

    Cross-Border Commercial Transactions

    • Drafting and negotiation of cross-border contracts
    • Technology transfer agreements (FITTA)
    • Mergers, acquisitions, and due diligence
    • Joint ventures and strategic partnerships
    • Export-import regulatory compliance

    International Employment & Immigration

    • Work permits and visa applications for foreign nationals
    • Employment contracts for expatriate staff
    • Compliance with Nepal's labor laws and regulations
    • Immigration advisory for foreign investors and entrepreneurs
    • Global mobility and secondment arrangements

    International Dispute Resolution & Regulatory Advice

    • Advice on cross-border disputes and enforcement mechanisms
    • Assistance with disputes involving foreign parties in Nepal's courts
    • International arbitration and mediation (see our Arbitration page)
    • Advice on international treaties and conventions
    • Tax and regulatory advice for foreign individuals and entities

    Local Counsel for International Law Firms

    • Representation of foreign law firms with clients in Nepal
    • Expert opinions on Nepalese law for international proceedings
    • Legal research and memorandum drafting
    • Assistance with document gathering and local procedures
    • Due diligence and compliance reviews for cross-border transactions

    Policy & Legislative Advisory

    • Analysis of new and proposed legislation affecting foreign entities
    • Advocacy on behalf of international clients in regulatory matters
    • Monitoring of legal and policy developments relevant to foreign business
    • Comparative law analysis for cross-border investments
    • Advice on international treaty obligations and domestic implementation
