Commercial Solutions

Corporate & Commercial Law

Bajra Law & Associates provides a full suite of services for businesses starting, operating, or scaling in Nepal. We help corporate clients navigate the complete business lifecycle, ensuring strict compliance with the Companies Act and Department of Industry guidelines.

We draft and negotiate commercial agreements, joint venture agreements, shareholder agreements, and distribution networks. Our client-focused approach guarantees that our legal frameworks match our clients' strategic and operational requirements.

  • Company registration and joint venture formation
  • Foreign Direct Investment (FDI) approvals
  • Corporate compliance and reporting
Dispute Resolution

Litigation & Arbitration

When disputes arise, our litigation team is ready to defend our clients' interests. We represent commercial entities across all tiers of the Nepalese courts, including the High Courts and the Supreme Court of Nepal.

We also advise on alternative dispute resolution mechanisms. Our team has extensive experience in domestic and international arbitration under UNCITRAL and local arbitration rules, focusing on resolving corporate disputes effectively and minimizing disruption.

  • Commercial and tax litigation
  • Domestic and cross-border arbitration
  • Enforcement of foreign arbitral awards
Financial Services

Banking & Finance

Our banking practice advises leading financial institutions, development banks, and corporate borrowers. We provide guidance on loan syndications, structured finance, project financing for infrastructure projects, and compliance with Nepal Rastra Bank directives.

We ensure that financing structures are fully compliant with Nepalese laws while protecting our clients' commercial and security interests.

  • Project finance and infrastructure lending
  • Foreign lending and ECB (External Commercial Borrowings)
  • Central bank compliance reviews
Corporate Growth

Mergers & Acquisitions

Bajra Law & Associates is a trusted advisor for complex corporate restructurings, mergers, and business acquisitions in Nepal. We conduct comprehensive legal due diligence, identify potential regulatory risks, and draft transaction documents.

We work closely with the Office of the Company Registrar, the Department of Industries, and sector-specific regulators to ensure a smooth transition and full compliance during transactions.

  • Due diligence audits and reports
  • Asset purchase and share purchase agreements
  • Antitrust and regulatory notifications
