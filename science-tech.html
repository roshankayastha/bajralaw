    At the Intersection of Law & Innovation

    We understand that technology moves faster than legislation. We help innovators, startups, and technology companies navigate the legal complexities of the digital age, protecting ideas, ensuring compliance, and enabling growth.

    Our practice is built at the intersection of law and innovation, offering strategic counsel across the full spectrum of technology law.

    A Note on Nepal's Evolving Tech Landscape

    Nepal is entering a new era of technology regulation. The Government of Nepal, at both the federal and local levels, have identified the information technology and innovation sector as a national priority. The government is actively shaping the future of digital commerce, data protection, intellectual property, and the broader technology ecosystem.

    We track these developments closely, ensuring our clients are prepared, compliant, and always ahead of the curve.
